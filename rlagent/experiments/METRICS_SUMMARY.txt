Common setup (all experiments):
  - Digital twin: sumotwin/7065.net.xml. Demand: sumodemand/{day}.rou.xml.
  - Action space: fixed 4-phase ring-barrier cycle
        EW_LEFT -> EW_THRU -> NS_LEFT -> NS_THRU (repeat),
        binary action per 1 s decision: EXTEND (hold) / ADVANCE (next phase).
  - Learner: linear FA, LSTDQ closed-form w=(A+ridge.I)^-1 b,
        ridge=1e-4, gamma=0.99/s, eps-greedy eps=0.05, semi-MDP reward.
  - Reward: -sum lane queue (veh < 5 km/h), summed per-second over a decision.
  - Training: full-day domain-randomized, day in {0505,0506,0507} x scale in
        [0.5,1.3]; 0508 HELD OUT for eval; sensor-outage bins masked; 20 eps.
  - Backend: libsumo (headless).

Metric definitions (metrics.py):
  - throughput = trips COMPLETED within the drained sim (day run to 30 h).
  - delay      = mean TOTAL delay = timeLoss + departDelay (s/trip).
                 departDelay = insertion backlog (spillback to source).
                 timeLoss-only (the fixed-2026-07-12 bug) hides oversaturation.
  - Lower delay is better. Low delay next to LOW throughput = WORSE
        (survivorship bias: gridlocked trips never enter the average).
  - v/c figures are peak-window degree of saturation (s=1900 veh/h/lane, GDOT).

--- EXP-003 §4c: numerical diagnosis (why the variance) ---
LSTDQ solve is ill-conditioned:
  feature mode      | cond(A)      | cond(A+1e-4 I) | ridge-sens.* | sym min-eig
  ------------------+--------------+----------------+--------------+-----------
  phase_gated       | inf (singular)| 1.9e10        | 0.27         | -2.8e3
  phase_gated_norm  | inf (singular)| 8.9e8         | 1.48         | -1.3e2
  phase_gated_v3    | ~1e69        | 3.7e8          | 1.50         | -1.2e2
  * fractional change in w when ridge goes 1e-4 -> 1e-2.
  - A is rank-deficient (ridge is load-bearing); solution ridge-determined,
    not data-determined -> big seed-to-seed variance -> parking lottery.
  - Normalization helps ~20x (1.9e10 -> 8.9e8) but doesn't fix it.
  - Weight signs: occupancy weights sensible (high queue -> extend, strongest
    EW_THRU ~1200-1600). Elapsed-time weight COUNTER-INTUITIVE (positive
    extend-preference = "the longer I've sat, the more I want to stay") ->
    self-parking; largest in exactly the parked phase of parked seeds.
